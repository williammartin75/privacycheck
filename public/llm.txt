# PrivacyChecker.pro

## About
PrivacyChecker is an automated GDPR, CCPA, and privacy compliance scanner for websites. It analyzes any website in under 60 seconds and provides a comprehensive privacy audit with an actionable compliance score.

## What it does
- Scans websites for cookie consent compliance
- Detects third-party trackers (Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, etc.)
- Checks privacy policy presence and completeness
- Verifies security headers (HTTPS, HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options)
- Detects dark patterns in consent banners
- Audits AI usage and disclosure
- Tests accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- Identifies applicable regulations (GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, POPIA, etc.)
- Provides a compliance score from 0 to 100

## Use cases
- Website owners checking their GDPR compliance before an audit
- DPOs (Data Protection Officers) performing pre-audit assessments
- Agencies auditing client websites for privacy compliance
- Developers ensuring their websites meet EU privacy standards
- Businesses assessing GDPR fine risk

## Key features
- Real-time scanning with Chromium browser engine
- Multi-page crawling (follows internal links)
- Dark pattern detection in cookie banners
- AI chatbot detection and disclosure audit
- GDPR fine risk simulator
- Consent Mode v2 documentation
- Blog with GDPR guides and compliance tips
- Available in 9 EU languages

## Technical details
- Built with Next.js 14, React, TypeScript
- Hosted on Render
- Uses Playwright for browser-based scanning
- Supabase for authentication and data storage
